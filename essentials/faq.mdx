<Accordion  title="What is Ground?">
Ground is an all in one Retrieval-Augmented Generation -- RAG.<br />
We handle chunking, embedding, and saving your documents to our vector database.<br />
You can then query the documents and get back a ranking of the best matches.
</Accordion>

<Accordion  title="Is it easy to setup?">
Once you're registered on [our platform](https://platform.ground.dev/dashboard).<br />
You can generate API Keys.<br />
These keys you get access to our endpoints for managing your documents and querying them with AI.
</Accordion>

<Accordion  title="Is there a chatbot?">
There is no chatbot.<br />
We only handle the document retrieval and ranking.
</Accordion>

<Accordion  title="How is the embedding done?">
We use a mixture of Dense and Sparse embeddings, then combine the search results with Reciprocal Rank Fusion (RRF)<br />
If you're interested to learn about the Reciprocal Rank Fusion, here is [an explanation](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/search/hybrid-search-ranking).
</Accordion>

<Accordion  title="What documents can Ground process?">
Ground can process PDFs, Word Documents, PowerPoints, Excel spreadsheets, emails, HTML, JSON, CSV, TSV, markdown, Open Office files, RST files, plain text files and XML.
</Accordion>
